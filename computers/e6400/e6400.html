This is a machine that I acquired in April of 2018, for the very gracious price of free! As of this writing in March of 2019, it is still in continuous use. That is set to change in the near future, however. I will be retiring this laptop, and replacing it with a new MacBook Air.

Specs
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo T9900 (upgraded from original C2D P8400)
  • Graphics Hardware: Nvidia Quadro NVS 160M (yes, it really has lasted this long!)
  • OS: Windows 8.1 (subject to change)
  • RAM: 6 GB
  • Storage: 80 GB Intel 320 Series SSD (upgraded from original 250 GB Seagate drive)
  • Backlit Keyboard

Along with the main unit, I also have the docking station, the "airbay", the DVD-ROM drive, the modular bay HDD, and the extended life 9-cell battery. I also installed the optional "swipe" style fingerprint reader.
